The Best AP Classes to Take, According to Jason

According to me, these are the best AP Classes of all time.

#3: AP Calculus AB/BC

This is a pretty fun class; the problems are simple and there's just a bit to memorize. However, once you understand the basics, the rest is easy.

#2: AP English Language & Composition

This class actually taught me a great deal about the elements of rhetoric and the strategies to construct a compelling argument. It's grueling, but one comes out stronger the other side.

#1: AP Chemistry

This class was plain fun. There was a mix of quantitative and qualitative analysis of the various parts of chemistry, mixed in with conceptual applications of various concepts too. You get a little bit of everything in that class.
